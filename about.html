About Harmony Music School Rwanda

Empowering Rwanda through the universal language of music since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Harmony Music School Rwanda was born out of a passion to bring high-quality music education to every corner of Rwanda. We strive to nurture talent, foster creativity, and inspire a love for music in all our students.

Our programs combine global music standards with the rich cultural heritage of Rwanda, ensuring students gain technical mastery while celebrating their roots. Through performance, collaboration, and mentorship, we empower the next generation of musicians and educators.

  • Discipline: Developing strong technical skills and performance ethics.
  • Community: Building connections and celebrating cultural pride.

Community Partnerships

We actively collaborate with schools, churches, cultural centers, and local organizations to promote music literacy and performance opportunities. Through workshops, concerts, and community events, we create platforms for young talent to thrive and communities to celebrate the power of music.
